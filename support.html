Support

Quality

The binaries that AdoptOpenJDK produces have passed the full OpenJDK test suite and other tests (donated by the community) ensuring a good quality binary. But not all binaries have passed the full JCK/TCK compliance testing. Binaries that have passed full JCK testing can be identified by the blue shield containing a check mark in their download button.

Community Support

The OpenJDK community does not offer any commercial support for the binaries produced here, but some vendors may offer commercial support for (some of) the binaries produced here.
